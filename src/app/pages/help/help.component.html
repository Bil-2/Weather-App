Help Center

Find answers to common questions

How accurate is the weather data?

Our weather data is sourced from OpenWeatherMap, one of the most reliable meteorological APIs. Data is updated every 10 minutes for maximum accuracy.

Can I search for any city worldwide?

Yes! WeatherApp supports weather searches for cities across the globe. Simply type the city name in the search bar.

How do I switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit?

Currently, temperature units are set automatically based on your location settings. Custom preferences will be available soon.

Is there a mobile app available?

WeatherApp is fully responsive and works perfectly on all mobile devices through your browser. A native app is in development.

How can I report an issue?

Please visit our Contact page to send us feedback or report any issues you encounter.

Weather Alerts Guide

Understanding severe weather warnings and safety

SEVERE WARNING

Red Warning

Take Action Immediately

Dangerous weather is expected or occurring. Monitor conditions and follow local emergency advice.

MODERATE WATCH

Orange Watch

Be Prepared

Conditions are favorable for severe weather. Stay informed and have a plan of action.

ADVISORY

Yellow Advisory

Be Aware

Less serious weather conditions are expected. Exercise caution during outdoor activities.

Note: Real-time alerts for your specific location are available on the Home page when active.
